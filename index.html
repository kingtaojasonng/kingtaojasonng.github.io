About Me

Currently, I am a Senior Data Scientist at ASIC working on machine learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) applications.

I recently completed my Master of Research program at Macquarie University under the guidance of Diego Molla-Aliod, Rolf Schwitter and Houying Zhu. My dissertation focuses on NLP.

Prior to this, I earned a Master of Data Science from the University of Sydney, working closely with Roman Marchant, Chao Sun, and Phil Henville on my dissertation project.

Additionally, I hold a Master of Economics from the University of Sydney. Alongside this, I completed a Master's in Information Technology and a Bachelor's in Computer Science, both achieved at the University of New South Wales.
