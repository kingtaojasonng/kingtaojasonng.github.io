About Me

I am a Senior Data Scientist at ASIC working on machine learning and natural language processing applications. Currently, I am a Master of Research/Doctor of Philosophy student at Macquarie University supervised by Diego Molla-Aliod and Rolf Schwitter. Previously, I obtained a Master of Data Science from University of Sydney under supervision of Roman Marchant, Chao Sun, and Phil Henville. To fulfil the requirements of the degree, I completed dissertation. I also received a Master of Economics from University of Sydney, and both Master of Information Technology and Bachelor of Science from University of New South Wales.
